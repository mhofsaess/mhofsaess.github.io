Our history

enviConnect was founded in 2020 by employees at the Stuttgart Chair for Wind Energy of the University of Stuttgart.

In March 2023 we were awarded an EXIST Business Start-up Grant from the German Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Climate Protection to develop a new wind lidar management tool.

In 2024, we spun off the apps and digitalisation group from enviConnect and now operate as a new, separate company under the name enerlace.

We wish Andy, Ines and John all the best.

Technology Transfer Initiative GmbH

We offer our services through Technologie-Transfer-Initiative GmbH.

TTI offers legal and accounting support for start-ups at the University of Stuttgart. This means that we can concentrate on our core competence with a professional team in the background - the customised planning and implementation of technical services for our customers in the field of measurement technology for renewable energies.
